Hen party A group of women coming together for a social occasion. Origin: It was surprising to find that the origin of “hen party” was most often available at wedding sites. The Scottish Tie the Knot explained the origin of the Ancient Greece ritual of a woman preparing to marry. The custom, much like now, was for the bride to spend time with female friends, family, and relatives preparing for the marriage. Later in the 1620s, “hen” was used as slang for a woman and in1800 a publication used ‘hen party” to describe a women’s gathering.
