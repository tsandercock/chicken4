You eat like a bird. A person who eats very small amounts. (Note: We recognize this is not true of chickens who always seem happy to eat and are constantly foraging.) The saying was used in the first half of the 1900’s, but most people recognize that a bird eats far more in relationship to it’s body as compared to that of a horse.
